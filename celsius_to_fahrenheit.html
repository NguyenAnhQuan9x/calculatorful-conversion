How to convert Celsius To Fahrenheit

If you want to calculate celsius to fahrenheit conversions manually, then this celsius to fahrenheit formula is should be taken into account. Here's the formula to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit:

°F=(°C×9/5)+32

Where:

  • °F - the unit temperature in Fahrenheit; and
  • °C - the unit temperature in Celsius.

For example, Let's use the formula to convert the boiling point of water, i.e., 100°C into Fahrenheit.

Placing the values in the first formula, we get:

°F=(100×9/5)+32=212

Thus, if we convert 100 °C into Fahrenheit, we get 212 °F.
