How to convert Centimeters To Millimeters

We can convert centimeter to millimeter by multiplying the length by the conversion ratio. as we known that one centimeter is equal to ten millimeters, So the formula can be:

mm = cm x 10

For example, Now that you know how to convert cm to m let's discuss examples. To convert 20cm to mm.

Placing the values in the first formula, we get:

mm = 20(cm) x 10 = 200

Thus, if we convert 20 cm into mm, we get 200mm.
