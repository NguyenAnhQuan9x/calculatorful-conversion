There are actually a few types of cups sued around the world, including the US legal cup, Imperial cup, Japanese cup, etc., and they all are different in terms of the value.

An Ounces known as the US fluid ounce, the unit of volume for liquid substances is used as ounce in the US and other countries practicing the US Customary system. How to convert cups to ounces

To convert one measurement from cups to ounces, you can use the below formula:

ounces = cups x 8.345404 x material density

The above formula can be hard to understand so let’s follow an example. You want to convert 5 cups to ounces with an ingredient density of 1 g/ml:
5 cups = 5 x 8.345404 x 1 = 41.72702 ounces
