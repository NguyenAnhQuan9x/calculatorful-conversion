First of all, you should know a little about the history of day and hour

Day is the amount of time needed for the Earth to complete one complete rotation around its axis. The time value is rounded up to 24 hours and is standardized. As a result, a day generally lasts 86,400 seconds. A day, denoted by the sign d, is the unit of time as it is used in the SI system. This unit is utilized extensively in a wide range of situations, from daily living to scientific requirements.

Hour is one of the commonly used units of measuring time, along with a second and a day. One hour is equal to 60 minutes, however, it will not be true to assume that an hour is equal to 3,600 seconds (according to the Universal Coordinated Time, every hour has so called leap second which can be either negative or positive). An hour is a unit accepted and widely used by all countries of the world, and it has got a symbol of h.

How to convert Days to Hours

The Earth rotates once in a full cycle every 24 hours, which is equivalent to one day. Therefore, a day is equal to 24 hours. The formula for is:

hours = days x 24

For example, Let's use the formula to convert 12 days to hr

Placing the values in the first formula, we get:

hours = 12 x 24 = 288

Thus, if we convert 12 days to hr, we get 288 hour
