How to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius

If you want to calculate fahrenheit to celsius conversions manually, then this fahrenheit to celsius formula is should be taken into account. Here's the formula to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius:

°C=(°F−32)×5/9

Where:

  • °F - the unit temperature in Fahrenheit; and
  • °C - the unit temperature in Celsius.

For example, Let's use the formula to convert the boiling point of water, i.e., 212°F into Celsius.

Placing the values in the first formula, we get:

°C=(212−32)×5/9=100

Thus, if we convert 212 °F back into Celsius, we still get 100 °C.
