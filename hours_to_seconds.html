First of all, you should know a little about the history of hours and seconds

Hour is one of the commonly used units of measuring time, along with a second and a day. One hour is equal to 60 minutes, however, it will not be true to assume that an hour is equal to 3,600 seconds (according to the Universal Coordinated Time, every hour has so called leap second which can be either negative or positive). An hour is a unit accepted and widely used by all countries of the world, and it has got a symbol of h.

Seconds is one of the fundamental time units in the SI. A second is now defined in terms of the time it takes for the Earth to revolve around the Sun, whereas in ancient times it was defined in terms of the length of a solar day. Atomic, electric, and mechanical methods can all be used to measure a second. A second is often denoted by the letter s and is equal to 1/60th of an hour.
