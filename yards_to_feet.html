Yards to Feet Conversion

Foot and yard is the two different units of measurement for distances that used in different situations. Both units are used in the imperial and US customary system of measurements. Although we frequently hear about measurements in feet, governmental sectors and road measurements prefer yard measurements. It is not difficult to convert between these two units.

How to convert Quarts to Ounces

If you want to calculate yards to ft conversions manually, then this yard to feet formula is should be taken into account. Yards can be multiplied by three to obtain the solution. Since one yard is equivalent to three feet, the conversion factor is three. It can be stated this way:

Feet = yards x 3

For example, you want to convert 5 yards to feet. You can multiply 5 by 3, and the answer is 15 feet
